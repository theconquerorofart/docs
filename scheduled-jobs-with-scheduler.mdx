You can schedule automation flows to run at specific times or intervals.

## Create a scheduled job

1. Ask the assistant to schedule an existing automation flow and describe when you want it to run.
2. The assistant creates a scheduled job backed by Chrome alarms.

Scheduling supports intervals, specific times, or run-at values.

## List scheduled jobs

Ask to see all active scheduled jobs and their next run times.

## Delete a scheduled job

Ask the assistant to delete a specific scheduled job.

## Requirements

The flow must already exist in Automation Hub before you schedule it. Scheduled jobs are managed from chat, not through the visual flow builder's trigger options.
