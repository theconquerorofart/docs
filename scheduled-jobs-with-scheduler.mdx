You can schedule automation flows to run at specific times or intervals using the `/scheduler` slash command.

## Create a scheduled job

1. Type `/scheduler` in the composer and describe when you want the flow to run.
2. The assistant creates a scheduled job backed by Chrome alarms.

Scheduling supports intervals, specific times, or run-at values.

## List scheduled jobs

Type `/scheduler` and ask to see all active scheduled jobs and their next run times.

## Delete a scheduled job

Type `/scheduler` and ask to delete a specific job.

## Requirements

The flow must already exist in Automation Hub before you schedule it. Scheduling is available through the slash command, not through the visual flow builder's trigger options.
