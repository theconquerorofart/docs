After forging custom skills, you can maintain them using `/tune` and `/prune`.

## Tune a skill

1. Type `/tune` in the composer.
2. Select the skill you want to update.
3. Describe what to change — rename it, refine its instructions, update parameters, or modify its code.

The assistant validates the updated skill before saving.

## Prune a skill

1. Type `/prune` in the composer.
2. The assistant shows a list of your forged skills and how often each has been used.
3. Select the skills you want to delete.

## Notes

Tuning and pruning only applies to skills you created with `/forge`. Built-in slash commands cannot be tuned or pruned.
