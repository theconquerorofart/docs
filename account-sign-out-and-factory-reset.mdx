## Account

Open **Settings** \> **Account** to view your profile, current plan, and access status.

## Sign out

Select **Sign Out** from Settings or the header account shortcut. This ends your session.

## Factory Reset

Factory Reset clears all extension data, including:

- Chat history and sessions
- Saved prompts
- Automation flows
- Forged skills and tool pairings
- System prompt
- Memory
- Settings and preferences

Provider keys stored in the vault are also removed. After a factory reset, you'll need to sign in again and reconnect your providers.

To perform a factory reset, open **Settings** and select **Factory Reset**. Confirm the action when prompted.
